## Step-by-Step: Creating Your Wyoming LLC from Morocco

### Step 1: Choose Your LLC Name

Your business name must:
- Be unique in Wyoming
- End with "LLC" or "Limited Liability Company"
- Not contain restricted words (Bank, Insurance, etc.)

**Tips for Moroccan entrepreneurs:**
- Check availability at Wyoming Secretary of State website
- Consider a name that works in both English and French
- Avoid names that are too similar to existing US companies

**Timeline:** Same day

---

### Step 2: Appoint a Registered Agent

Wyoming requires every LLC to have a registered agent - a person or company that receives official mail on your behalf.

**What a registered agent does:**
- Receives legal documents
- Forwards mail to your Moroccan address
- Maintains compliance with Wyoming state

**Cost:** $49-199/year (included in our formation packages)

**Timeline:** Same day

---

### Step 3: File Articles of Organization

This is the official document that creates your LLC. It includes:
- LLC name
- Registered agent information
- Business address
- Member/manager information

**Filing fee:** $100 (state fee)

**Timeline:** 1-3 business days for standard filing, same day for expedited

---

### Step 4: Get Your EIN (Employer Identification Number)

Your EIN is like a social security number for your business. You need it to:
- Open a US bank account
- Apply for Stripe
- File US taxes
- Hire employees (in the future)

**How to get an EIN without SSN:**
- File Form SS-4 with the IRS
- Indicate "Foreign" in the SSN field
- Submit via fax (fastest) or mail

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks via fax, 4-8 weeks via mail

**We handle this for you in our formation packages.**

---

### Step 5: Create an Operating Agreement

While not legally required in Wyoming, an Operating Agreement is essential for:
- Defining ownership percentages
- Outlining management structure
- Protecting your limited liability status
- Opening bank accounts (many banks require it)

**What to include:**
- Member names and ownership percentages
- How profits/losses are distributed
- Management responsibilities
- Voting rights

**Timeline:** 1-2 days

---

### Step 6: Open a US Business Bank Account

This is where many Moroccan entrepreneurs get stuck. Here's how to do it:

**Option 1: Mercury (Recommended for Tech/Online Business)**
- 100% online application
- No US address required
- Accepts Moroccan passport
- Free business checking
- API access for developers

**Option 2: Relay (Great for E-commerce)**
- Online-only banking
- Integrates with Shopify, Amazon
- No minimum balance
- Cashback on debit card

**Option 3: Wise Business**
- Multi-currency account
- Hold USD, MAD, EUR
- Great for international payments
- Lower fees than traditional banks

**Documents needed for any bank:**
- Articles of Organization
- EIN confirmation letter
- Passport
- Operating Agreement
- Proof of address (Moroccan utility bill works)

**Timeline:** 1-14 days

---

### Step 7: Apply for Stripe

Stripe allows you to accept credit card payments from customers worldwide. Many Moroccan entrepreneurs create a US LLC specifically to access Stripe.

**Requirements for Stripe:**
- US LLC (Wyoming works great)
- EIN
- US bank account
- Website demonstrating your business
- Valid passport

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake 1: Not Filing Form 5472
**Consequence:** $25,000 penalty
**Solution:** File every year, even with $0 income

### ❌ Mistake 2: Using a Fake US Address
**Consequence:** Bank account closure, legal issues
**Solution:** Use your real Moroccan address

### ❌ Mistake 3: Not Keeping Records
**Consequence:** Problems during audits
**Solution:** Use accounting software from day one

### ❌ Mistake 4: Mixing Personal and Business Funds
**Consequence:** Loses limited liability protection
**Solution:** Separate business bank account
