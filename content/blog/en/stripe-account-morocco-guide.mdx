## Can You Get Stripe in Morocco?

**Short answer: Yes, but not directly.**

Stripe is not available in Morocco as a supported country. However, Moroccan entrepreneurs can access Stripe through a legitimate method: creating a US LLC.

### The Solution: Create a US LLC

- Form a Wyoming LLC
- Get EIN from IRS
- Open US bank account
- Apply for Stripe with US entity

**This guide shows you exactly how.**

## Step-by-Step: Getting Stripe from Morocco

### Step 1: Create Your US LLC

**Recommended: Wyoming LLC**

Why Wyoming:
- Lowest fees ($62/year)
- No state income tax
- Privacy protection
- Fast formation (1-3 days)
- Works perfectly with Stripe

**Cost:** $100-300 depending on service

**See our guide:** [How to Create a Wyoming LLC from Morocco](/blog/wyoming-llc-morocco-complete-guide)

---

### Step 2: Get Your EIN

Your EIN (Employer Identification Number) is required for:
- Stripe application
- US bank account
- Tax filings

**How to get EIN without SSN:**
1. Complete Form SS-4
2. Write "Foreign" in SSN field
3. Fax to IRS: +1 (855) 641-6935
4. Wait 2-4 weeks

---

### Step 3: Open a US Bank Account

Stripe requires a US bank account for payouts.

**Best Options for Moroccans:**

| Bank | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| **Mercury** | 100% online, no fees | May require video call |
| **Relay** | E-commerce friendly | Newer bank |
| **Wise Business** | Multi-currency, low fees | Not full US bank |

**Documents needed:**
- LLC Articles of Organization
- EIN confirmation letter
- Passport
- Operating Agreement
- Proof of address

### Step 5: Apply for Stripe

Once you have:
- ✅ US LLC documents
- ✅ EIN
- ✅ US bank account
- ✅ Professional website

**Apply at:** https://dashboard.stripe.com/register

**Application Tips:**

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use business email | Use personal Gmail |
| Match LLC name exactly | Use different business name |
| Provide real Moroccan address | Use fake US address |
| Answer all questions honestly | Leave fields blank |

---

### Step 6: Verification Process

Stripe will review:
- Business information
- Website legitimacy
- Owner identity (passport)
- Bank account ownership

**Timeline:** 1-7 days typically

**Possible Outcomes:**

| Result | What Happens |
|--------|--------------|
| **Approved** | Full Stripe access |
| **Pending** | Additional documents needed |
| **Restricted** | Limited functionality |
| **Rejected** | Must reapply or appeal |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it legal to use Stripe from Morocco?

**Yes, if you have a legitimate US LLC.** Using a US entity to access Stripe is a common and legal practice.

### How long does the whole process take?

**4-8 weeks total:**
- LLC formation: 1-2 weeks
- EIN: 2-4 weeks
- Bank account: 1-2 weeks
- Stripe application: 1-7 days

### Can I use a friend's US address?

**No.** Stripe verifies your identity. Use your real Moroccan address for the beneficial owner information.

### Do I need to pay US taxes on all my income?

**No.** Only US-sourced income is typically taxable. Income from Moroccan customers usually stays in Morocco's tax jurisdiction.
